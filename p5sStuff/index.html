Persona 5 Scramble Shenanigans

Persona 5 Scramble is a spinoff of Persona 5 that currently has yet to be released outside of Japan. There are some ways you can legally obtain it, and there are some tricks that you can do aswell.

Getting the game (Switch)

Go to https://accounts.nintendo.com and make a new account, MAKE SURE YOUR LOCATION IS SET TO JAPAN Once that is done, go make a new profile on your Switch

Link the account you just made to that profile

Login to the eshop with that profile

Success! You can now access Japanese eshop, now just find Persona 5 Scramble and either install the demo or buy the game.

Stuff

Obviously once the full game comes out outside of Japan, there should be more stuff here.

In the beginning area, if you don't preform the showtime attack, you can play the demo infinetly! Or atleast the starting area, so you can fight all the shadows you want.
